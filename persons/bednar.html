Lyn Ulbricht (USA)
Freedom advocate and mother of Silk Road founder Ross Ulbricht

Lyn has become a full-time activist since her son Ross, the alleged creator of Silk Road, an anonymous online marketplace operated as a Tor hidden service, was arrested by the US security services. She has been working around the clock to help Ross by running the FREE ROSS ULBRICHT fund and raising various issues concerning freedom of internet which surrounded the trial which led to his life sentence.

Lyn se stala aktivistkou na plný úvazek po té, co jejího syna Rosse, tvůrce a provozovatele Silk Road - anonymního on-line tržiště provozovaného v prostředí tzv. “deep web”, zatkly americké bezpečnostní složky. Pracuje nepřetržitě ve snaze pomoci Rossovi k čemuž vede fond FREE ROSS ULBRICHT, prosazuje témata týkající se svobody na internetu a spochybňuje růné okolnosti procesu, které vedli k doživotnímu trestu pro jejího syna.
